Equipping Spirit-filled Leaders for Ministry Excellence

Explore Courses Learn More

Our Mission

To train and equip Spirit-filled men and women for effective ministry in the body of Christ, preparing them to serve God with excellence and integrity in the Pentecostal tradition.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of biblical education that produces godly leaders who will impact their communities and nations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit.

About Evangel Bible College

Evangel Bible College is a Spirit-filled institution of higher learning committed to training men and women for ministry in the Pentecostal tradition. As part of the Assemblies of God General Council, we maintain the highest standards of biblical education while emphasizing the gifts and ministries of the Holy Spirit.
