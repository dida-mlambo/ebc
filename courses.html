Our Courses

Comprehensive biblical education programs designed to equip you for ministry

Certificate in Biblical Studies

A foundational program covering essential biblical knowledge and ministry principles for those beginning their theological education.

Course Curriculum:

  • Old Testament Studies
  • New Testament Studies
  • Christian Doctrine
  • Church History
  • Homiletics (Preaching)
  • Ministry Practicum
  • Pentecostal Theology
  • Christian Ethics
1 Year Program
Full-time / Part-time

Entry Requirements:

  • High School Certificate
  • Born-again Christian testimony
  • Church recommendation letter

Diploma in Theology

Advanced theological training with emphasis on Pentecostal doctrine, pastoral ministry, and spiritual leadership.

Course Curriculum:

  • Systematic Theology
  • Biblical Hermeneutics
  • Pentecostal Studies
  • Pastoral Theology
  • Church Administration
  • Counseling & Care
  • Worship & Music Ministry
  • Missions & Evangelism
2 Year Program
Full-time

Entry Requirements:

  • Certificate in Biblical Studies or equivalent
  • 2 years ministry experience
  • Pastor's recommendation

Ministry Leadership

Specialized training for church leadership, focusing on practical ministry skills and organizational development.

Course Curriculum:

  • Leadership Principles
  • Church Growth Strategies
  • Financial Management
  • Team Building
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Youth & Family Ministry
  • Community Outreach
  • Ministry Innovation
1.5 Year Program
Full-time / Part-time

Entry Requirements:

  • Basic theological qualification
  • Leadership experience
  • Church endorsement

Missions & Evangelism

Comprehensive preparation for cross-cultural ministry, church planting, and evangelistic outreach worldwide.

Course Curriculum:

  • Cross-Cultural Studies
  • World Religions
  • Evangelism Methods
  • Church Planting
  • Missionary Preparation
  • Cultural Anthropology
  • Language Learning
  • Mission Strategy
2 Year Program
Full-time

Entry Requirements:

  • Diploma in Theology or equivalent
  • Missionary calling testimony
  • Medical clearance
