About Evangel Bible College

Equipping Spirit-filled Leaders for Ministry Excellence

Our Mission

To train and equip Spirit-filled men and women for effective ministry in the body of Christ, preparing them to serve God with excellence and integrity in the Pentecostal tradition.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of biblical education that produces godly leaders who will impact their communities and nations with the Gospel of Jesus Christ through the power of the Holy Spirit.

Our Story

Evangel Bible College is a Spirit-filled institution of higher learning committed to training men and women for ministry in the Pentecostal tradition. As part of the Assemblies of God General Council, we maintain the highest standards of biblical education while emphasizing the gifts and ministries of the Holy Spirit.

Founded with a vision to equip leaders for the Kingdom of God, our college has been serving the body of Christ by providing comprehensive theological education that combines academic excellence with practical ministry experience.

Biblical Foundation

Solid biblical education rooted in Pentecostal doctrine and the authority of Scripture

Spirit-Filled Ministry

Emphasis on the gifts and fruits of the Holy Spirit in ministry and daily life

Practical Training

Hands-on ministry experience and practical application of biblical principles

Academic Excellence

High-quality education that meets international theological standards

Our Heritage

Founded on Biblical Principles

Evangel Bible College was established with a clear mandate to train Spirit-filled leaders for the advancement of God's Kingdom. Our foundation is built on the solid rock of Scripture and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Assemblies of God Legacy

As part of the Assemblies of God General Council, we carry forward a rich heritage of Pentecostal faith and practice. Our commitment to biblical truth, spiritual gifts, and evangelistic fervor shapes every aspect of our educational approach.

Commitment to Excellence

We believe that God deserves our very best. This commitment to excellence permeates our academic programs, faculty development, student services, and ministry preparation. We strive to graduate leaders who will make a lasting impact for the Kingdom of God.

Our Campus Facilities

Library & Study Center

Our comprehensive theological library houses thousands of books, journals, and digital resources. Quiet study areas and group discussion rooms provide ideal environments for academic research and spiritual reflection.

Modern Classrooms

State-of-the-art classrooms equipped with multimedia technology, comfortable seating, and excellent acoustics. Each room is designed to facilitate interactive learning and meaningful discussion.

Chapel & Prayer Room

Our beautiful chapel serves as the spiritual heart of the campus. Daily chapel services, prayer meetings, and special events take place in this sacred space dedicated to worship and spiritual growth.

Student Accommodation

Comfortable dormitory facilities provide a safe and conducive environment for students. Shared and private rooms are available, fostering community while respecting individual needs.

Dining Hall

Our dining facility serves nutritious meals prepared with care. The communal dining experience strengthens relationships and provides opportunities for fellowship among students and staff.

Technology Center

Computer lab with high-speed internet access, research databases, and digital learning platforms. Students have access to online theological resources and modern educational technology.

Accreditation & Recognition

Academic Standards

Evangel Bible College maintains high academic standards and is committed to providing quality theological education that meets international benchmarks. Our programs are designed to prepare graduates for effective ministry and further academic pursuits.

Assemblies of God Endorsement

As an institution under the Assemblies of God General Council, our programs are recognized and endorsed by one of the world's largest Pentecostal organizations. This endorsement ensures our graduates are well-prepared for ministry within the Assemblies of God and beyond.

Ministry Preparation

Our graduates serve in various ministry roles across Zimbabwe and internationally. Many have gone on to plant churches, lead congregations, serve as missionaries, and establish other ministry organizations that advance the Kingdom of God.
