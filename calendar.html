Important Notices

Registration Reminder

All students must complete their registration before the semester begins. Late registration may incur additional fees.

Fee Payment

Semester fees must be paid in full before attending classes. Payment plans are available for qualifying students.

Textbook Orders

Course textbooks are available at the college bookstore. Pre-order to ensure availability at semester start.
